About

Blood Trends v1 · All records are AES-256-GCM encrypted with a key derived from your PIN (PBKDF2, 310,000 iterations) and stored only in this device's browser storage. The hosted site serves code only; no data ever leaves your phone. If you forget your PIN there is no recovery — restore from an exported backup instead.
